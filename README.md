# CHAIN MAIL &#128232;

A 90s chain letter simulator. Forward or delete? Keep the chain alive or face the consequences.

## Play

[**Play now**](https://ashleywolf.github.io/chain-letter-game/)

## About

Remember getting chain letters in your AOL inbox? The ones that threatened 7 years of bad luck if you didn't forward them to 10 friends? This is that, but as a game.

You'll navigate a fake 90s email client from 1995 to 1999, deciding which chain letters to forward and which to break. Your choices affect your curse meter, luck score, and whether your Tamagotchi survives.

## Features

- Authentic chain letter templates (Bill Gates money, missing children, clown statue, angel wishes)
- AOL-era email client UI with Comic Sans and everything
- Curse meter that triggers bad luck events
- Address book that grows as you progress through the years
- Dial-up connection intro
- Multiple endings based on your choices
